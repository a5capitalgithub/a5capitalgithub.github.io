The A5 Team


Donato Adellini

President & CEO

Danny is a business executive with significant business management experience. Danny is able to work through difficult situations, identify key risks and opportunities missed by other lenders, negotiate effectively and produce successful outcomes for investors.

Danny was a founder and CEO of one of Canada’s fastest growing waste management businesses. After merging his business with Green For Life Environmental (“GFL”), Danny successfully sold his stake in GFL and with the proceeds from the sale, began investing in commercial mortgages. Since 2011, and prior to founding A5 Capital Inc., Danny has successfully invested on behalf of himself and his investors, generating significant returns.

Email: ardellini@a5cap.com


John Paterson

Junior Partner

John sources and structures new transactions and provides due diligence and detailed mortgage investment analysis prior to funding mortgages.

From 2011 to 2013, John was an Investment Analyst in the Mid-Market Private Debt Team within Private Fixed Income (“PFI”) at Sun Life Assurance Company of Canada. He was responsible for the analysis required to underwrite new US and Canadian corporate debt transactions, lease and loan securitizations, private equity fund commitments and portfolio management presentations and reports. John graduated with Distinction from the Richard Ivey School of Business at The University of Western Ontario and has completed two levels of the CFA curriculum.

Email: paterson@a5cap.com
